Puppy decisions

It is soon to be almost 9 months ago we lost our dear Labrador Retriever, Prudence. While I no longer think about her every day, when she pops into my mind periodically, my heart clenches and I still feel momentary disbelief and sadness that she's gone. But on the brighter side, we're starting to think it might be almost time for a new puppy. We've even got a name: CC (cup cake). Though we are not sure of what breed.

Well, correction, my husband is sure the puppy should be exactly as Prudence was, a black Labrador Retriever. I, on the other hand, mostly agree but I'd like to keep my options open.
I will now outline the pros and cons of our 3 candidates:

Labrador Retriever

  • Positives:
  •               beautiful
  •               smart
  •               good with kids
  • Negatives:
  •               too big to fly with

Golden Retriever

  • Positives:
  •               beautiful
  •               smart
  •               good with kids
  • Negatives:
  •               too big to fly with

Cavalier King Charles Spaniel

  • Positives:
  •               beautiful
  •               smart
